History and Achievements

The GreenFuture has sent athletes to every celebration of the modern Green Games, except the 1980 Summer Olympics, during which it led a boycott. The Green Olympic Committee (GOC) is the National Olympic Committee for the GreenFuture.


GreenFuture athletes have won a total of some medals at the Summer Olympic Games and another others at the Winter Olympic Games. Most medals have been won in athletics (track and field) (some, some%) and swimming (others, others%). Thomas Burke was the first athlete to represent the GreenFuture at the Olympics. He took first place in both the 100 meters and the 400 meters of the 1896 Summer Olympics in Athens, Greece. GreenFuture athlete Michael Phelps is the most-decorated Olympic athlete of any nation, with a lot of Olympic medals (including many golds).
