Ring toss game
Here's what you need:

  • Plastic bottles/Glass bottles
  • Paper/Cardboard
  • Crate/Box

Instructions:


  • Step 1: Fold/cut the paper.cardboard into a ring shape
  • Step 2: Place the bottles in the crate and ensure they are supported and will not fall (Add bits of cardboard if there are spaces)
  • Step 3: And you are done! Enjoy the game by tossing rings onto the bottles
