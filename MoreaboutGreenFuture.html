More about GreenFuture

" At their core, organisations are just a giant network of relationships. So if you fail to build those relationships, your chances of succeeding are not very high. " This is a quote borrowed from Quote Master, and these words define our very purpose and who we strives to become.

We are a non-profit organisation whose aim are shared together with our friend, Claire, who strongly believe in contributing to make the envrionment a better place. We are made up of a group of like-minded individuals, who feels compelled to do more to save the environment.

Events, Community-sessions, Voluntary Initiatives. That are the " stars " which the organisation revolves around! However, to actually turn it into a successful organisation, we will definitely need you and your help, to participate in such activities, to turn this world better, and to build a green future!
